BizTrip


BizTrip was a design challenge I did to create a prototype for an app that helps business travelers manage their trips. The entire product design from start to finish was completed in 3 days.


Product Design | UX | UI

Problem


Business travelers need better a better way to organize all aspects of their travel from their itinerary, to contacts and communication in the area of travel.

Users


The target users of BizTrip are those who travel frequently for work in sales/ consulting oriented roles. BizTrip focuses on travel trends of young, millennial business travelers.

Design


Inspiration


Due to the limited time frame for this design, I relied on inspiration from many applications whos features and designs helped make BizTrip.
