lessons learned

  • details matter - we learned in testing our initial prototype that the details of language used, placement of buttons, and overall structure we key for an optimal usability experience.
  • listen to user intuition - In our heuristics evaluation, we found that our users’ intuition led them to attempt to use our application in a way other than we intended. Our initial reaction was to guide the user to how we wanted them to accomplish tasks, but we ultimately learned it was best to design based on the users’ intuition to increase discoverability and usability.
  • thinking beyond visual design - User experience encompasses more than the look and feel of an application. This project challenged us to think about other aspects of designing an application for a user, such as data privacy, ethics, and possible legality issues.
