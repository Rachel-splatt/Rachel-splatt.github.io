beautibook

BeautiBook is my Computer Science entrepreneurial capstone project. My partner and I are aiming to create an inclusive social platform made specifically for the beauty community to share their work. We aim to give users a fast but effective way to share and store their makeup looks. Beautibook will be a more informative option for users to discover new looks, displaying products used, look diagrams, and steps. Currently, we’re working on idea testing and building the foundations of our business. Next semester, We’ll be diving into IOS development using Swift. Stay tuned, and check us out at beautibook.github.io/app
